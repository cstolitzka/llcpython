Focused on mobile commerce, the team supports mobile browser experiences across platforms and shopping applications (apps) on Smartphones and tablets including BlackBerry, Android phones, iPhone, Windows Phone 7, Kindle Fire, Android tablets, and iPad. Although the primary execution focus of this role is on the phone applications, the mobile team is a multi-platform, global organization and the senior product manager will be expected to lead strategic thinking, innovation, and initiatives that extend across the entire organization.
